About me

My name is Victor Ramos Alves de Matos, I'm an accountant currently transitioning into data analytics, based in Juiz de Fora, MG, Brazil. I hold a degree in Accounting Sciences from UFJF, am a licensed accountant, and am currently pursuing a degree in Systems Analysis. I have been working in accounting since 2019, focusing on fiscal obligations, bank reconciliation, and financial reporting across multiple client companies. Over time, I have combined my accounting background with data skills — building automated pipelines, structured spreadsheets, and BI dashboards — and I am currently transitioning into data-focused roles at the intersection of finance and technology.
